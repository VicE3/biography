Victoria Echevarria Biography

Childhood

I was born in New Jersey. Most of my youth was spent moving between North Carolina and Florida. Memories of Florida are among my fondest. There is nothing like stepping out your front door and seeing gators, snakes, turtles, and pigs.

Education

After high school, I atttended Central Piedmont Community College for one year. I put college on pause with the intention to return once I had enough money to afford it.
